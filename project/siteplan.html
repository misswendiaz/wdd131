WDD 131 Project: Tutor Buddy Site Plan

Site Name

Tutor Buddy

Tutor Buddy is your teaching partner — a helpful website that makes it easy for tutors to track lessons, students, schedules, and progress. It's designed to support tutors so they can focus more on teaching and less on managing paperwork.

Site Purpose

The site serves as an admin tool for tutors to log their sessions, manage their schedule, and track their billing — all in one place.

Scenarios

  • What is Tutor Buddy?
  • Who is Tutor Buddy for?
  • Do I need to create an account?
  • Can I edit a session after logging it?
